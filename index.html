dMorph for World of Warcraft - Wrath of the Lich King 3.3.5а

Dmorph it's program for change your appearance for version 3.3.5a 12340rev.

Use the chat of the game to enter commands.

©DDS (Zdohdds - https://worldofwarcraft.com/ru-ru/character/soulflayer/здохддс)

WARNING!!!
Only be run in window mode
To morph into another race (Human, in this case): 
.race 1
Race IDs: 
  1. Human
  2. Orc
  3. Dwarf
  4. NightElf
  5. Undead
  6. Tauren
  7. Gnome
  8. Troll
  9. Goblin
  10. BloodElf
  11. Draenei
WARNING!!! for the next race haven't models without gear:
- Dwarf Female
- NightElf Female
- Draenei Male
- Undead Male
- Undead Female
- Troll Female
To swap genders after a race morph: 
.gender
To morph an item (where 1 is the Head): 
.item 1 2955
inventory slots: 
    1 -- head
2 -- neck
3 -- shoulder
4 -- shirt
5 -- chest
6 -- waist
7 -- legs
8 -- feet
9 -- wrist
10 -- hands
15 -- back
16 -- main hand
17 -- off hand
18 -- ranged
19 -- tabard
To morph yourself into the Thrall: 
.morph 4527
To scale yourself: 
.scale 0.4
WARNING!!!
For entering floating point numbers use the sign point (use a decimal point, not a decimal comma)

Default value 1.0
